Frequently Asked Questions

What size dumpster do I need?

Estimate your debris volume and type, then compare to common sizes like 10-, 20-, 30- or 40-yard bins.

How long can I keep the dumpster?

Standard rental spans 7–10 days, but extensions are available for short-term or long-term projects.

What items are prohibited?

Hazardous materials, tires, large quantities of liquids, and certain electronics are typically not allowed.

Does the dumpster fit my driveway?

We’ll review your site access and space before delivery to ensure a suitable placement.

Do I need a permit?

If placing the dumpster on public property (street/sidewalk), check local regulations in USA. On private property, usually no permit is needed.

East Los Angeles, CA Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

East Los Angeles (Spanish: Este de Los Ángeles), or East L.A., frequently recognized by locals as "East Los," this region is an unincorporated area situated within Los Angeles County, California, United States. According to the United States Census Bureau, East Los Angeles is designated as a census-designated place (CDP) for statistical purposes. The most recent data from the 2020 census reports a population of 118,786, reflecting a 6.1% decrease compared to the 2010 population of 126,496.
